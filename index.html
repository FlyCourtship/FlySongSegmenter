Stern Lab: Fly Song


  


Multi-channel recording and automated segmentation of Drosophila melanogaster courtship song. BJ Arthur, T Sunayama-Morita, P Coen, M Murthy, and DL Stern (in prep).  PDF


The raw data below is stored in lossless .wav files with the sampling rate embedded. Single-precision floating-point values correspond to the voltage output of the microphones and include a 500x gain. Mating pairs are grouped by strain and compressed to the following .zip archives.

    Antigua.zip (210 MB)

    Beltsville.zip (260 MB)

    CS-Tully.zip (449 MB)

    Ore-R.zip (228 MB)

    Taiwan.zip (210 MB)

Open-source song segmentation code for Matlab is hosted by GitHub:

    FlySongWMTSegment
