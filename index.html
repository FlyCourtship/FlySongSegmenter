Courtship Song Recorder and Fly Song Segmenter

Multi-channel acoustic recording and automated analysis of Drosophila courtship song.
BJ Arthur, T Sunayama-Morita, P Coen, M Murthy*, and DL Stern*.   *co-corresponding authors
BMC Biology (in press).  PDF

Open-source schematics for the electronics and courtship chambers:

    CourtshipSongRecorder

Open-source song segmentation software:

    FlySongSegmenter

Raw data is stored in lossless .wav files with the sampling rate embedded. Single-precision floating-point
values correspond to the voltage output of the microphones and include a 500x gain.
